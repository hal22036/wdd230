The North Las Vegas Chamber of Commerce

Overview

Site Purpose

The North Las Vegas Chamber of Commerce is here to help promote and protect the interests of local businesses in the community. We are a group of business owners that support each other through networking and advocating for one another.

Scenarios

  • What are the fees and requirements to be involved with the chamber?
  • What committees are currently available that will advocate for my business?
  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
