Why join the Vegas Chamber?

Membership in the Vegas Chamber sends a powerful message that your business is connected to a larger network of organizations within our community dedicated to ensuring Southern Nevada is a great place to live, work, and visit. We are the voice of business – and together, our voice shapes policy, creates opportunities, and makes Southern Nevada “home.” Joining the Chamber and becoming part of the largest business support organization in Nevada has never been easier! There are several packages that fit your business goals and budget, and we invite you to browse them below: To find out what level best suits your business, please give us a call at 702.641.5822 to connect with a member of our business development team. The Vegas Chamber’s member engagement team is there to guide you through the membership process, as well as ensuring you realize value on your membership. Your member engagement liaison will help you sign up for cost-saving benefits, events, and make recommendations on how to gain ROI from your membership investment. They are available to help anytime you have a question, request, or need additional information. Thank you for your membership and investment in the Southern Nevada community!
